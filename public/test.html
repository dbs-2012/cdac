Step

Step 1 : Input the values of x and y coordinates of the three vertices of the triangle in the specified fields.
Step 2 : Once all coordinates have been entered click on the draw triangle button to get the triangle on the graph.
Step 3 : Click on the points of the base of the triangle to get a line parallel to the base.
Step 4 : Drag the line drawn on the base parallel to base to divide the sides of the triangle.
Step 5 : Click on the Use scale button to get the scale on the graph.
Step 6 : With the help of the scale measure the sides of the triangle bisected by the parallel line to the base and note them down.
Step 7 : With the noted down the lengths of the bisected sides calculate the ratio.
Step 8 : Use the Calculate ratio button to get the ratio and compare the correctness of the one calculated by you.
